## Overview

`<justifi-season-interruption-insurance>` offers insurance upsells within Modular Checkout. It displays coverage highlights, premium amounts, and collects opt-in consent before continuing payment.

## Usage

<UsageSnippet
  title="Attach insurance upsell"
  code={`<justifi-modular-checkout
  account-id="acc_123"
  auth-token="wct_checkout"
  checkout-id="chk_456"
>
  <justifi-season-interruption-insurance slot="addons" />
  <justifi-modular-checkout-card-form slot="payment-methods" />
  <justifi-modular-checkout-summary slot="summary" />
</justifi-modular-checkout>`}
  language="html"
/>

### Events

- `selection-changed`: Emits whenever customers toggle the opt-in checkbox.

### Public methods

1. `select()` / `deselect()` – Programmatically toggle the add-on.

## Theming & Layout

- Use CSS vars like `--justifi-addon-surface` and `--justifi-addon-border` to align with your brand.
- The component fits best in a column that is 320–480px wide; keep copy concise to avoid overflow.

## Accessibility

- Checkbox uses a native `<label>` for high click targets and includes `aria-describedby` pointing to coverage text.
- Premium amounts are read aloud alongside the coverage summary so the upsell is clear.
