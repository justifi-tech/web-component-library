## Overview

`<justifi-sezzle-payment-method>` surfaces Sezzle as a Buy Now, Pay Later option alongside cards and bank payments. It handles eligibility checks, displays required disclosures, and redirects customers to Sezzle for authorization before returning to the checkout flow.

### Events

- `sezzle-redirect`: Fires before redirecting the customer to Sezzle.
- `sezzle-returned`: Emits when the customer comes back to the checkout with approval data.
- `error`: Surfaces issues retrieving Sezzle offers or redirect URLs.

### Public methods

1. `begin()` – Trigger Sezzle flow programmatically (useful for custom list UI).

## Theming & Layout

- Respect Sezzle’s branding by keeping the provided colors and logos intact. Surrounding padding can be customized with CSS props like `--justifi-rail-spacing`.
- Provide at least 320px of width for the disclosure copy to avoid wrapping mid-word.

## Accessibility

- Buttons include descriptive `aria-label` text specifying Sezzle as the selected payment method.
- Disclosures use `<small>` text with accessible contrast ratios and link semantics.
- Focus returns to the component after the customer finishes the Sezzle redirect to keep context.
