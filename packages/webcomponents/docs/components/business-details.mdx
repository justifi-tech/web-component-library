## Overview

`<justifi-business-details>` walks operators or merchants through the information required to underwrite a business: legal name, EIN, addresses, and principals. Use it in onboarding flows where you need a guided, validated experience that syncs directly with JustiFi APIs.

## Usage

<UsageSnippet
  title="Business intake step"
  description="Pass the account context plus the business you want to edit."

### Events

- `submitted`: Fires when all sections validate and emit an updated business payload.
- `error-event`: Emits when API requests fail or validation errors occur; payload includes field-level hints.

### Public methods

1. `submit()` – Programmatically trigger validation and submission.
2. `reset()` – Clear the form back to the most recently loaded business payload.

## Theming & Layout

- Use CSS custom properties like `--justifi-form-surface`, `--justifi-form-border-radius`, and `--justifi-form-spacing` to align with your brand.
- The component targets a comfortable width between 320px and 640px; beyond that, content centers to maintain readability.

## Accessibility

- All fields ship with associated `<label>` tags and `aria-describedby` hints for inline validation.
- Error summaries focus the first invalid input and announce via `role="alert"`.
- Keyboard users can tab through sections without encountering hidden controls.
